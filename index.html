India: An Overview

  • World's largest democracy with over 1.4 billion people
  • Located in South Asia, bordered by Pakistan, China, and Bangladesh
  • Federal republic with 28 states and 8 union territories
  • Capital: New Delhi, Financial hub: Mumbai

Rich Cultural Heritage

  • Home to 22 official languages, Hindi and English most widely spoken
  • Birthplace of four major religions: Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism
  • UNESCO World Heritage Sites: Taj Mahal, Red Fort, Ajanta Caves
  • Ancient traditions in art, music, dance, and philosophy dating back 5000 years

Economic Powerhouse

  • World's 5th largest economy by nominal GDP
  • Major IT and software services hub, often called "Silicon Valley of Asia"
  • Leading producer of spices, tea, textiles, and pharmaceuticals
  • Space program achievements: Mars mission, moon landing, satellite launches
