India Overview

  • World's largest democracy - 1.4B people
  • Located in South Asia
  • 28 states, 8 union territories
  • Capital: New Delhi

Cultural Heritage

  • 22 official languages
  • Birthplace of 4 major religions
  • UNESCO sites: Taj Mahal, Red Fort
  • 5000+ years of traditions

Economic Power

  • 5th largest economy globally
  • IT hub - "Silicon Valley of Asia"
  • Leading spices & pharma producer
  • Space missions: Mars, Moon
